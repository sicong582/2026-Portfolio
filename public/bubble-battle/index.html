Bubble Battle

How to Play

  • Goal. Clear every mushroom. Each bomb blows after a short fuse (~0.9s; try ?fuse=2 for slower rounds); flames go one tile up/down/left/right (plus shape) — avoid them. If a blast hits you, the round restarts with a new field.
  • Controls. WASD or arrows to move. Space plants a bomb (up to six; chains allowed).
  • View. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom. P and G switch views. Use Hide or H to collapse this panel — tap How to Play to open it again.
